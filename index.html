About Me

Sofia Fernandez has been working in the information technology industry for more than 12 years as a Technical Writer. Her experience includes developing and managing various internal and external documentation in small, medium, and large companies. She also has experience working directly with internal and eternal teams from Executives, Engineers, Developers, Quality Assurance Teams, Product Managers, Translators, Sales, Marketing, Support, and others.

Aside from her passion in technical communication and information technology, her other interest include video games, guitars, theater, photography, knitting, and martial arts.
